## What is blinqr?

blinqr is an open-source reminder device that helps you stay on top of daily tasks. It features 5 programmable reminder slots, each with its own LED and physical button for acknowledgment.

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="5 Reminder Slots" icon="list-format">
		Configure up to 5 independent reminders with custom names, icons, and schedules.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Physical Feedback" icon="approve-check">
		LEDs light up when reminders fire. Press the corresponding button to acknowledge.
	</Card>
	<Card title="BLE Connected" icon="puzzle">
		Connects to a companion mobile app via Bluetooth Low Energy for easy configuration.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Open Source" icon="github">
		Firmware is fully open source. Build your own, modify it, or contribute improvements.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
